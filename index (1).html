Welcome to the DARK Series

DARK is a Netflix original series that premiered in 2017, created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese. The show is set in the fictional German town of Winden, where a missing child sets off a chain reaction of events that unravels a complex web of secrets, lies, and supernatural mysteries spanning three generations. The show explores the profound question: Is everything connected?

Character Introductions

Jonas Kahnwald: A central figure who uncovers the mysteries of time travel and his role in the cycles.

Martha Nielsen: Jonas's love interest, whose fate is tied to the multiverse's unraveling.

Ulrich Nielsen: A police officer determined to solve the case of missing children, whose past actions have major consequences.

Claudia Tiedemann: The enigmatic "White Devil," who works to break the cycles of time travel manipulation.

Noah: A sinister priest working to manipulate the time loop for his own gains.
